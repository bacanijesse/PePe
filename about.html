About Pedal & Peak

  • Pedal & Peak is a personal cycling and hiking journal created to document rides, hikes, photos, route notes, videos, and outdoor stories in one organized place.
  • This site focuses on real adventure logs, including distance, elevation, moving time, route memories, trail lessons, and the small details that make every trip worth remembering. Some routes are peaceful, some are challenging, and some make me seriously question my life decisions halfway up the climb.
  • The Adventures section keeps ride and hike cards organized, while the Testimonials section highlights the people, stories, and shared experiences connected to each journey.
  • As this project grows, Pedal & Peak may include interactive maps, GPX uploads, route guides, gear notes, embedded videos, and longer blog-style stories from different adventures. For now, it is built as a personal passion project - powered by curiosity, perseverance, and the occasional need to stop and catch my breath.
  • Whether you are a fellow cyclist, hiker, outdoor enthusiast, or simply someone looking for route ideas, I hope this site gives you useful insights, inspiration, and maybe a small reminder that the best views usually come after the hardest climbs.
  • Thanks for visiting, and see you on the next ride, hike, or emergency snack break.
